These Terms of Service govern your use of the Fit-to-Token Chrome extension.

1. Description of the service

Fit-to-Token is a Chrome extension that overlays the ChatGPT web interface with an ambient eco-feedback widget. It connects physical activity data, such as energy or calorie expenditure, to a replenishable prompt-energy allowance that is spent by prompting and recharged through activity. The extension is intended for personal reflection, research, and educational feedback.

2. No medical advice

Fit-to-Token is not a medical device and does not provide medical, health, fitness, nutritional, or exercise advice. Any activity, calorie, and energy calculations shown by the extension are estimates and should not be used for medical decisions.
